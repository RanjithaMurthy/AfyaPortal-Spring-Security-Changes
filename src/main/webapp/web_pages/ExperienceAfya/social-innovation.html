Afya Arabia - revolutionizing the regional healthcare landscape through social innovation in the healthcare ecosystem

Social innovation is a powerful approach that can transform the way healthcare is delivered and create a deep impact on the entire society. This innovation shapes healthcare delivery by establishing a collaborative network among the three pillars of healthcare - patients, care-providers and payers. Afya's social innovation initiative brings together all the stakeholders of the healthcare ecosystem and engages them through healthcare, information technology and mutually beneficial relationships to enable better care.

The innovative ecosystem

This social innovation is truly the future care where, within an ecosystem, healthcare is indeed 'local' - that is, accessible by all the members with ease, penetrable across the three pillars and efficiently delivered to the people in a secure environment. This innovative ecosystem enables well-informed citizens to take right decisions about healthcare.
