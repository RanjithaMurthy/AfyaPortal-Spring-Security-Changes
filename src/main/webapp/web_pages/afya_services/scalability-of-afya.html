Home Afya Services Scalability of Afya

The healthcare landscape is constantly evolving; hence all innovative initiatives must definitely plan for scalability right from the initiation stage. Afya's ecosystem has built in systems that can adapt to change and support the continuing innovations in the field of healthcare in terms of business, technology, functionality and infrastructure.

Building Community

Afya Arabia has started building the smart care community from Kuwait, and our aim is to build such smart care communities for each country by widening our presence in the MENA region. But our aim is to expand our geographical reach and widen our healthcare network. This growth would definitely require a constant focus on adapting to changing needs, resource management, tapping potential areas of development, meeting the unique requirements of people belonging to different geographies, scaling up operations to match the rate of growth and maintaining exemplary standards.

Functionality

Afya aims to extend its reach to include more stakeholders in the healthcare ecosystem such as general hospitals, rehabilitation centres, super-specialty hospital, para-medical professionals, medical gadget and device providers. Hence our constant endeavour is to work on an approach that supports a scalable functionality. Healthcare innovations are the order of the day and drive new trends in care delivery. Afya's functional expertise is set up to adapt to these innovations and new technologies. We understand that every innovation that simplifies healthcare and ensures high quality care experience is a catalyst to achieve the aim of wellness for all.

Technology

One of the factors that guarantee a successful implementation of such a complex ecosystem is a robust technological backbone. With the ever-continuous advances in technology from information exchanges to electronic medical records and SaaS-based medical practice management, Afya's primary focus is on creating a system that can easily adapt to technological innovations that simplifies human intervention in healthcare.

Infrastructure

Afya's ecosystem is tuned to easily incorporate multiple users and applications. A multi-tier architecture is adopted to streamline applications, data and web services. Supporting technical infrastructure is adequately deployed to handle higher bandwidth, increasing users and advanced storage needs. Further, the design of the ecosystem also facilitates smooth integration of additional applications and devices as and when they are available.
