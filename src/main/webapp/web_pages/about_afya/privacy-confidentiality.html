3. Cookies and other technologies

3.1. A cookie is a small amount of data that is placed in the browser of your computer or on your mobile device. This privacy policy applies to cookies and similar technologies including pixel tags and web beacons (collectively referred to as "cookies").

3.2. The reason afya arabia uses cookies is because web pages do not have memory. If you are surfing from page to page within a website, you will not be recognised as the same user across pages. Cookies allow you to be recognised as the same user across the pages of a website. Cookies also allow your choices to be remembered such as the language you prefer, the currency you use and your search criteria. They will also make sure you are recognised when you return to a website. The cookies afya arabia use has varying lifespans. The maximum lifespan afya arabia sets on some of them is five years from your last visit to our website. You can erase all cookies from your browser any time you want to. Aafyaaarbia uses the services of trusted and recognised online advertising and marketing companies. Afya arabia may also use third-party providers for analytical purposes. To enable their services, these companies need to place cookies. The providers we use are committed to building consumer awareness and establishing responsible business and data management practices and standards.

3.3. Using your browser settings in, for example, internet explorer, safari, firefox or chrome, you can set which cookies to accept and which to reject. Where you find these settings depends on which browser you use. Use the "help" function in your browser to locate the settings you need. If you choose not to accept certain cookies, you may not be able to use some functions on our website. However, we do not otherwise support "do not track" browser settings. Opting out of an online advertising network does not mean that you will no longer receive or be subject to online advertising or marketing analysis. It means that the network from which you opted out will no longer deliver ads tailored to your web preferences and browsing patterns.

3.4. Afya arabia's website uses the following types of cookies:

3.4.1. Technical cookies Afya arabia tries to give you an advanced and user-friendly website, products and services that adapts automatically to your needs. To achieve this, afya arabia uses technical cookies to show you afya arabia's website, to make it function correctly, to create your user account, to sign you in and to manage your account. 3.4.2. Functional cookies Afya arabia also use functional cookies to remember your preferences and to help you to use afya arabia's website, products and services efficiently and effectively, for example by remembering your preferred currency and language, your searches and the products and services you searched and/or viewed earlier. 3.4.3. Analytics cookies Afya arabia use these cookies to gain insight into how our users use the website, to find out what works and what does not, to optimise and improve the website and to ensure afya arabia continues to be useful and relevant. The data afya arabia gathers includes which web pages you have viewed, which referring/exit pages you have entered and left from, which platform type you have used, date and time stamp information and details such as the number of clicks you make on a given page, your mouse movements and scrolling activity, the search words you use and the text.You type while using the website. 3.5. Afya arabia has access to its cookies. Cookies placed by third parties can be accessed by these third parties.
