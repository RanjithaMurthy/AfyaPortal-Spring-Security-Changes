Overview


Afya Arabia, as a pioneer in bringing social innovation in the field of healthcare in the MENA region, anticipates the changing trends in the healthcare sector and adopts a holistic approach in transforming the way healthcare is delivered.


Afya Arabia is an elaborate healthcare ecosystem that encompasses Patients, Care-providers and Care-payers. The objective of this ecosystem is to provide an exclusive platform that brings together all stakeholders of care under a common roof and help them achieve synergistic advantages by rendering, seeking, optimizing and customizing healthcare services.

Leadership

Afya Arabia is a Kuwait-headquartered company whose primary objective is care for all, wellness for all, healthy workforce, increased employability and healthy citizens. Under Afya's leadership, this venture promotes an innovative initiative to build a healthcare ecosystem for the Arab world that will have a strong connect with all the stakeholders as a secured, trusted and regional healthcare service provider.

Afya Arabia's team comprises a close-knit group of experts who are passionate in their endeavour to make this social innovation in healthcare a reality. A unique initiative like Afya requires expertise from varied domains such as healthcare, technology, management and applications, insurance and socio-economic sciences. Afya's leadership includes leaders from all these domains and are further supported by advisory councils of leading doctors, insurance specialists, visionaries, strategists, futuristic consultants, ICT consultants and training consultants who guide Afya in shaping the ecosystem to revolutionize the healthcare landscape. Thus Afya's social innovation is the dream that is being shaped into reality under the governance of an able team of business leaders and champions from the industry.
