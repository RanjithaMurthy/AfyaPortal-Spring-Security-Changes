Afya's Policy to Care Providers  
Smart Solutions Policy
1 Time Period Trial period starts from the time member subscribes for Afya Services.
Scope of Afya Services during Trial Period - All Services included in the selected pack are available for effective use during Trial Period.
Trial period will be valid for 30 days from date of subscription.
When the member subscribes during the trial period, the member gets the benefit of the complete trial period, as the subscription days are counted from the date of on completion of Trial Period.
Trial period is not applicable for any future subscription renewals or upgrades. This is an initial one-time facility provided to the member.
2 Subscription A member can subscribe by making on-line payment through www.afyaarabia.com's payment gateway.
The subscription fees has to be paid in advance for either half yearly or an annual subscription - as per members choice.
A subscription once made, cannot be cancelled, transferred or exchanged with any manner.
Kindly accept the 'Subscription Agreement' as directed to on Afya's portal.
3 Additional Training Service Upon subscribing, the member is requested to ensure that the users undergo self learning sessions to get themselves acquainted and trained for the effective usage of Afya services.
Afya will provide fixed hours of online support, to the users, while they train by themselves.
The member has the facility to request for Additional Training Services on Afya, if the same is needed.
Additional Training Services once booked & paid cannot be cancelled / transferred or exchanged with any other services of Afya.
4 Notification Pack A member needs to subscribe for the Notification Pack, if the member wants to send SMS notification, that are enabled by Afya's services, as a part of optimizing the members workflows.
Notification pack is not required if only emails are to be sent.
Afya provides the facility to its members to have a personalised touch to the SMS, that are enabled through Afya. For this, the member needs to state the Sender ID, while registering or it can be updated subsequently. However, till the member does not provide the Sender ID, the member still enjoys the privilege of sending SMS with the Sender ID as 'afyaarabia'.
5 Renewal In order to ensure smooth operations and continued services of Afya, the member is adviced to renew their subscription any time before the active subscription expires.
Upon renewal, the member would enjoy the privileges & benefits as per Afya's policy prevailing at the time of renewal. Like-wise the members responsibility & the charges payable would be as applicable, as per the Afya's policy prevailing at the time of renewal.
The member has the option to redeem the loyalty points, if any, while renewing the subscription.
If the member has loyally points and the member does not renew the services, then Afya will provide the member the option to request to convert the loyalty points thereby extending the validity of the services, by a proportionate number of days. However, if the member does not request such conversion and if the membership expires, then the loyalty points get forfeited.
Upon discontinuation of Smart Solutions Service, the Smart Services facility would be stopped 7 days in advance.
Once the subscription is expired due to non renewal, the Premium member becomes a Community Member.
The Community Member would be able to login to the portal & download his DB backup only, within 15 days of expiry.
For any reason, if the member is unable to renew the subscription on time, but later decides to re-join Afya's community, then the member is most welcome back to Afya Community Care. In such event of a break and rejoining thereafter, the member can choose to be considered as a new member or to request for the past data, while continuing the services. Afya will be able to provide the past data with a additional fees 1) for only activating the masters or 2) activating with all past records. Re-training, will be charged separately. The re-activation fees does not include free re-training.
  Smart Services Afya Policy for Care Provider
1. Appointment Premium - Doctors Appointment Booking is possible only 7 days in advance.
Cancellation by Patient - Can be done upto 48 hrs prior to appointment time. Afya will refund 100% to the Patient in 10 days from cancellation
Cancellation by Clinic/Doctor - On behalf of Clinic / Doctor, Afya will refund Full advance to the Patient. Value Added Fees for this transaction will be deducted while making payment to the Clinic/Doctor.
Rescheduling by Patient - Can be done until 48 Hrs prior only.
Rescheduling by Clinic / Doctor - If the Patient agrees for the rescheduling proposed by the Clinic/Doctor, then rescheduling by the doctor is valid. Else, this would be considered as cancellation by Clinic/Doctor. Thus, on behalf of Clinic / Doctor, Afya will refund Full advance to the Patient. Value Added Fees for this transaction will be deducted while making payment to the Clinic/Doctor.
No Show of Patient - The advance is non-refundable to the Patient.
No Show of Doctor (not communicated to Patient) - 100% advance is to be refunded to the Patient by the Clinic/Doctor. The Patient would be expected to collect the refund while visiting the Clinic.

Afya’s Smart Services are for Cash Paying Patients only.
