Home Members Area

Patient

As a patient in Afya's ecosystem, you can enjoy many benefits that simplify your care experience at the click of a button. You will join our ecosystem as a community member. You will have access to a personalized dashboard that includes a range of reports and trend charts (e.g., spend summary, visit summary, insurance approval status and membership usage, trend charts of visits, types of health complaints, etc.). You can also avail a range of smart services such as online appointment booking for clinic visit or for home visit with your preferred care provider, tele-consultation facilities, and home pharmacy. The most useful feature is the repository of every single previous and current care visit detail (e.g., prescriptions, care provider's notes, diagnostic test reports, etc.). We welcome you to enjoy the empowering experience awaiting you as a member of Afya's ecosystem.

Care Provider

Care providers can choose from two membership options - community and premium. Care providers become community members on registering with Afya. The members have access to a dashboard that provides a 360-degree overview of the care provider's business within the ecosystem (e.g., statistics of interactions with patients, clinics, and other stakeholders). The care provider member area also includes "My Network" - where the entire care provider's member network details are available online. The premium membership requires payment of a subscription fee and includes additional packages that simplify the care provider experience. "Loyalty point" is a unique value addition. With Afya's membership care providers can enjoy an organized, well-informed care experience. We invite you to explore our membership services.
