Afya’s Policy for Smart Services: Patients  
Appointment Premium – Doctors Policy
1 Appointment Booking The Patient can book for a Premium Appointment 7 days in advance from the booking time.
2 Cancellation by Patient Cancellation of an appointment can be done only 48 hrs prior to appointment time. Upon cancellation, on behalf of the Clinic/Doctor Afya will refund the 100% amount paid by the Patient. The Patient can view the status of refund by logging in to “My Account”.
3 Cancellation by Clinic/Doctor If the Clinic/Doctor cancel the appointment then on behalf of the Clinic/Doctor Afya will refund 100% advance to the Patient in 10 days from the date of cancellation. The Patient can view the status of refund by logging in to “My Account”.
4 Rescheduling by Patient The Patient can reschedule an appointment 48 hrs prior to the appointment time.
5 Rescheduling by Clinic / Doctor The Clinic/Doctor can reschedule the appointment 48 hrs prior to the appointment time. If the Patient agrees for the rescheduling proposed by the Clinic/Doctor, then rescheduling by the doctor is deemed valid. Else, Afya would consider this as “Cancellation by Clinic/Doctor”. Then on behalf of the Clinic/Doctor Afya will refund 100% advance to the Patient in 10 days from the date of cancellation. The Patient can view the status of refund by logging in to “My Account”.
6 No Show of Patient If the Patient does not visit the Clinic/Doctor on the appointment date & time, this will be considered as “No Show” of the Patient & and the advance is non-refundable.
7 No Show of Doctor (not rescheduled by communicating with Patient) If the Doctor is not available for Consultation on the Appointment date and if this is appointment was not rescheduled by communicating with the Patient by the Clinic/Doctor, then Afya treats this as “No Show” by the Doctor and 100% advance is to be refunded to the Patient by the Clinic/Doctor. The Patient needs to collect the refund amount, from the Clinic.

Tele Consultation
Tele consultation is available through Afya App installed on Smart Phone which is downloadable from Google Play & Apple App Store, Afya enables a service to book an Appointment with the Care Provider for Tele consultation.

Afya App enables by reminding the appointment time. The Patient can initiate the Tele consultation to by dialing through the Smart Phone i.e using the present GSM/CDMA facility rendered by your telecom operator.
1 Appointment Booking The Patient can book for a Tele Consultation 7 days in advance from the booking time.
2 Cancellation by Patient Cancellation facility is not available for Tele Consultation.
3 Cancellation by Clinic/Doctor Cancellation facility is not available for Tele Consultation.
4 Rescheduling by Patient The appointment can be rescheduled only 24 hrs prior to the appointment time.
5 Rescheduling by Clinic/Doctor The Clinic/Doctor can reschedule the appointment 24 hrs prior to the appointment time. If the Patient agrees for the rescheduling as proposed by the Clinic/Doctor, then rescheduling by the doctor is deemed valid. Else, Afya would consider this as “Cancellation by Clinic/Doctor”. Then on behalf of the Clinic/Doctor Afya will refund 100% advance to the Patient in 10 days from the date of cancellation.
6 No Show of Patient If the Patient does not initiate the call for Tele Consultation on the scheduled time or any time within the booked slot, this will be considered as “No Show” of the Patient. The advance is non-refundable.
7 No Show of Doctor (not rescheduled by communicating with Patient) If the Doctor does not respond to the Patient’s call during the booked slot, and if the Clinic/Doctor does not reschedule the appointment by communicating to the Patient, then it is considered as “No Show”. Patient needs to write to Afya and on confirmation from Clinic/Doctor, On behalf of the Care Provider Afya will refund 100% advance to the Patient.
